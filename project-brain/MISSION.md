# Mission & Vision

## Who We Serve

Home maintenance contractors in the Atlanta metro area. Specifically:
- Roofers — residential roof repair, replacement, inspection
- Painters — interior/exterior residential painting
- HVAC Technicians — heating, ventilation, AC installation and repair
- Plumbers — residential plumbing repair and installation
- Electricians — residential electrical work
- General Contractors — multi-trade home maintenance and renovation

These are small business owners and skilled tradespeople. Most run crews of 1-10 people. They are experts in their craft but often struggle with the business side: finding leads, managing the pipeline, invoicing, marketing, and client communication.

## The Problem

1. Contractors waste time driving around looking for work or relying on word-of-mouth
2. They use paper invoices, text messages, and spreadsheets to manage their business
3. Marketing feels foreign and expensive — they don't know where to start
4. Homeowner communication is disorganized — missed calls, lost emails, no project visibility
5. They don't know which neighborhoods have the most opportunity based on home age and maintenance cycles

## Our Solution

A single platform that combines:
- Lead intelligence — subdivision data showing where homes need work based on age, mapped visually
- Pipeline management — from lead to completed job, tracked visually
- Professional invoicing — create, send, and track invoices with PDF generation
- Client portal — give homeowners a professional window into their project
- Marketing tools — email campaigns, templates, and marketing plan guidance
- Project management — organize every job with timelines, photos, notes, and change orders

## What Success Looks Like

- A contractor opens the app in the morning and knows exactly where to focus
- They can go from "I found a lead" to "invoice paid" without leaving the platform
- Their clients think they're working with a much bigger, more organized company
- They're winning more jobs because they found the right neighborhoods at the right time
- They spend less time on paperwork and more time on the tools and skills they love

## Design Philosophy

- Respect the user's intelligence — they're experts in their field, not ours
- Respect the user's time — every click should earn its place
- Respect the user's context — they're on a roof, in a crawl space, in a truck. Mobile-first, always.
- Look professional — this app represents their business. It should feel like it costs more than it does.

## Revenue Model

Tiered subscription:
- Starter — basic tools for solo contractors just getting organized
- Professional — full platform for growing businesses
- Enterprise — team features, API access, custom branding for established companies
