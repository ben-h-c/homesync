## Technical Discoveries

### Payment Processing (Stripe)
- **Credit card fees**: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **ACH Direct Debit**: 0.8% capped at $5.00 per transaction
- **Cost comparison**: $1,000 invoice costs $29.30 via card but only $5 via ACH
- **New (2025)**: Stripe charges $15 dispute counter fee + 30% success fee on Smart Disputes
- **Best practice**: Offer ACH for large invoices (>$500), cards for small. Clear payment terms in every invoice.
- Source: [Stripe ACH docs](https://docs.stripe.com/invoicing/ach-direct-debit), [Stripe fees guide](https://hostmerchantservices.com/articles/stripe-fees-a-pricing-guide-to-stripe/)

### Email Benchmarks (2025-2026)
- Average open rate: **43.46%** (inflated by Apple Mail Privacy Protection)
- Average click rate: **2.09%**
- Click-to-open rate: **6.81%**
- **Best practice**: Focus on click-through rate, not open rate (Apple MPP makes opens unreliable)
- Consulting/professional services benchmark: 45.96% open, 2.41% click
- Source: [MailerLite benchmarks](https://www.mailerlite.com/blog/compare-your-email-performance-metrics-industry-benchmarks), [ActiveCampaign benchmarks](https://www.activecampaign.com/glossary/email-marketing-benchmarks)

## Marketing & Growth Research

### SaaS User Acquisition (2025-2026)
- **SEO** is highest-ROI channel: Average CAC $205, 60% lower than paid search, 75% lower than LinkedIn ads
- **Product-Led Growth (PLG)**: Give buyers product access early — free trials, interactive demos
- **Referral programs**: Turn satisfied customers into acquisition channels
- **Interactive onboarding**: Guided tours + milestone nudges = fastest time-to-value, lowest churn
- Target **3:1 LTV:CAC ratio** as healthy benchmark
- Source: [Monday.com SaaS playbook](https://monday.com/blog/marketing/saas-marketing-strategy/), [Powered by Search](https://www.poweredbysearch.com/learn/saas-growth-strategy/)

### Content That Reaches Contractors
- **Educational blog posts**: "Which neighborhoods in [city] need roof work this year?"
- **Free tools**: Maintenance cost calculators, seasonal checklists
- **Local SEO**: "HVAC contractor software Atlanta" → landing pages per metro
- **YouTube**: Contractors watch tutorial and review content
- **Trade shows**: HVAC/R Expo, Roofing Expo, local home shows
- **Referral incentives**: $100-500 credit for successful referrals (standard in contractor SaaS)

### Email Campaign Best Practices
- **Best send times**: Tuesday/Wednesday 9-11am
- **Personalization**: Include subdivision name in subject line (feels local, not mass)
- **Subject lines**: Specific data > generic ("60% of homes need roof work" > "Maintenance opportunity")
- **Frequency**: Bi-weekly campaigns recommended; more frequent = higher unsubscribe
- **Template types that convert**: Cold outreach with data, seasonal reminders, post-project thank you + referral ask
